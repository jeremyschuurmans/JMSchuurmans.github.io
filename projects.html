Companies I've Helped
  • Handshake

    The Problem

    An innovative company working to help college students by democratizing access to job opportunities (and swiftly becoming the go-to resource for job-seeking students) needed help with a game-changing front end redesign.

    The Solution

    In this highly collaborative effort, I worked closely with other engineers and designers to create a sleek and intuitive experience for users. I took an active role in our team's Agile process and culture by leading scrum meetings and retros, and hosting a skribbl.io team game.

    Technologies Used

    Ruby on Rails, React, Redux

  • ThermoFisher Scientific

    The Problem

    A top laboratory equipment manufacturer needed help iterating and improving a successful internal sales tool.

    The Solution

    Here's something you don't see every day: a Ruby on Rails app with a MongoDB database. As a solo developer on this fascinating project, I integrated an existing serial number lookup tool into their app, delivered initial stages of a custom CMS, including full-stack development of an admin screen feature, and a customizable alert bar. Have gained a thorough understanding of their data model, I also helped consult on a migration of their database from MongoDB to PostgreSQL.

    Technologies Used

    JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, MongoDB, Azure

  • GoLogic Solutions

    The Problem

    An exciting startup providing Chicago-area businesses with a pickup and delivery solution in the midst of the pandemic needed help realizing their broader vision for their React Native mobile app.

    The Solution

    My first task on this project was to increase test coverage on their Ruby on Rails API. Then, I implemented several front end and back end improvements, including adding PostGIS for a geolocation feature, and driving a substantial front end redesign. Among the improvements I delivered were a brand new landing page, filtered search results, and an improved shopping cart experience, helping to make the founder's vision for their app a reality, and support user growth outside of their Chicago-area base.

    Technologies Used

    Ruby on Rails, React Native, Heroku
